My favorite character in Animal Farm is Boxer(A fictional character created by George Orwell in the book Animal Farm because I think that he is loyal, hard worker, and brave. In the book, Boxer have 2 mottos or principle. One of them was "I will work harder!"(Animal Farm) and after Napoleon(Fictional character created by George Orwell in the book Animal Farm) became the leader of the Animal Farm, he added another motto or principle which is "Napoleon is always right"(Animal Farm). This shows he is a hard worker, and also represents his loyalty to Animal Farm and to the leader, regardless it is Snowball(A fictional character created by George Orwell in the book Animal Farm or Napoleon as leader.

He is also brave, because of this quote in the book "the most terrifying spectacle of all was Boxer, rearing up on his hind legs and striking out with his great iron-shod hoofs like a stallion"(Animal Farm), this quote shows that even when he is facing danger he did not act like a coward, instead he fight like a brave man.

In conclusion, so many details shows that Boxer is a hard worker, loyal, and brave. But it did not go well at the end. This is why that he is my favorite character. R.I.P Boxer
