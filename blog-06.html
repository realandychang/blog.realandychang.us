Create account and Post articles

Written by Andy Chang

Video

Hello fellows, this is the first tutorial article! If you want to watch the video instead, go ahead! In this article, I will be teaching you how you can make an account and post articles!

Account Create

To create an account, you will need to email me with few things! First, make sure you send it to the right user! The email you are going to send to is andyc@realandychang.us! The second step you would want to do is type in the correct format (Available at the format section). After that, you may send the email to me. Just to let you know that I can not reply immediately, but I will make sure to do it as soon as possible!

Post Articles

After you created the account and got a reply, you can now post an article! Go to the navigation bar and click on the "Contact" button once more. This type you want to follow the format for Post Article format (Available at the formate section). When you completed the article typing, send the email (After you can create an account, your email will add to our starred list).

Format

-Account Format

Subject: Account Create

Body Text:

Account Name: (Type in the username you want to be displayed publically)

Account Profile Picture: (Attach it by naming the attachment profile.png/jpg/jpeg)

Account Social Media: (This will be visible on your profile card page, make sure to type in two URLs. You will get a notification if your social media link is not supported)

Account contact Email Address: (Please include this label! Leave this blank if you want to use the email address that is sending this email)

-Post Article Format

Subject: Post Article

Body Text:

Account Name: (Type in the one you used for registration)

Article Title: (The title will display on your article page, it would be /your-username/articles)

Article Body Text: (Paste the article you wrote down into the body text section, be sure to format correctly)

Ending(OPTIONAL): (It would be something at the end that you want to share the main idea or a couraging word!)

Back-up Plan

If you do not want to send us an email on article posting, you may submit a Google Form, which is only allowed once a week. If you want to edit the article again, you will be required to send us an email!

Link: https://forms.gle/S53vRC9wU83hiwgt6
